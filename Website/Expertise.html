With the aim of establishing ourselves as a firm and trusted company in the country, Dralion provides a comprehensive range of specialized construction solutions and services for a wide range of projects. These are briefly shown below.

EARTHWORKS

In any kind of construction, earthwork is one of the most essential and also the most expensive components of the project. Bearing that in mind, we offer our service to provide the most convenient and cost-effective earthwork solutions and ensure the flow of project tasks and activities are facilitated smoothly.

REINFORCEMENT WORK

With our highly skilled and experienced teams, reinforcement work for any kind of projects can be completed within time. We can offer our clients an efficient reinforcement work solution for fabrication, bending and binding at site of steel reinforcement.

FORMWORK

Good formwork greatly reduces the amount of time it takes to construct a project by minimizing construction cycle time. Our formwork system and skilled team can provide superior quality and environment friendly formwork solutions for all kinds of concrete structures.

CONCRETE WORK

The most important ingredient for the integrity of a structure is the quality of concrete works. Hence, we offer to undertake the construction of concrete structures and deliver the project while maintaining quality, integrity and sustainable service. We strive to provide value and satisfaction to all our clients.

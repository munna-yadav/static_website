GENERAL INFORMATION

Dralion Builders set out itself in pursuit of becoming a reputed and trusted construction company in Bhutan. With the goal of delivering quality projects, we work towards excelling in the field of Civil and Structural Engineering. Dralion Builders was founded to meet a growing demand for specialized construction services in Bhutan. Responding to rapid demands in the construction and building industry, Dralion is to develop into a customer focused solution provider for all types of building and construction projects.

OUR MISSION

Dralion aims to deliver any project at its finest completed state while ensuring quality, safety and environment friendly construction solution to all our clients. We work towards safely delivering any project, in any environment on time and within budget to benefit our customers, shareholders, employees and the communities we serve.

OUR VISION

To be established as one of the most prominent and trusted construction company in Bhutan by delivering “Quality and Value” construction solutions to all our clients.

CORE VALUES

The quality of a person being sincere, faithful, and truthful in appearance, speech, and action is what fuels the successful delivery of any project. At Dralion, we have strongly inherited integrity as we strive to establish ourselves as one of the most reliable construction company in the industry.

TEAMWORK AND DIVERSITY

Diversity in a workplace draws on a full range of skills, abilities and experiences. Our multicultural workforce encourages innovative thinking, fosters decision making and ensures greater flexibility in executing complex projects. We strive for a common goal with unity in diversity.

COMPLIANCE

While we undertake a variety of construction projects, we ensure that the requirements of the local and international standards are met. As we commit ourselves to continuous growth, we pledge that all business activities carried out will be in compliance with the local and international practicable law and maintain profound health, safety and environment friendly standards.

CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT

We greatly believe that improvement is a never-ending process and are committed to the refinement of not only the quality of projects we deliver but also to the growth and prosperity of our diverse teams. We are devoted in providing value to our clients while fulfilling the aspirations of our teams.
